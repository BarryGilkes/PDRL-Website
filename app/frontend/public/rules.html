Rules & Safety

PDRL rules are designed to be clear, fair, and easy to follow. Safety is always the top priority.

📋 General Rules

  1. All participants must sign a waiver before competing.
  2. Valid driver's license required for all drivers.
  3. Vehicles must pass tech inspection before racing.
  4. No alcohol or drugs permitted on the racing premises.
  5. Helmets are mandatory for all drivers (Snell SA2015 or newer).
  6. Fire extinguisher must be accessible in all race vehicles.
  7. Seatbelts/harnesses must be worn at all times on the track.
  8. PDRL officials have final say on all rulings and disputes.
  9. Unsportsmanlike conduct will result in immediate disqualification.
  10. Staging: both drivers must be staged within 15 seconds of the tree being activated.

🛡️ Safety Requirements

All Classes

  • DOT-approved helmet (Snell SA2015+)
  • Closed-toe shoes
  • Long pants required
  • Working seatbelt/harness
  • Battery tie-down

10.0 and Faster

  • SFI-rated roll cage
  • Fire suit (SFI 3.2A/1 minimum)
  • 5-point harness
  • Window net (driver side)
  • Master kill switch

⚖️ Tech Inspection

All vehicles must pass tech inspection before competing. Inspections cover:

  • Fluid leaks (oil, coolant, transmission, fuel)
  • Battery securement
  • Throttle return spring functionality
  • Wheel lug nut torque
  • Safety equipment verification
  • Class compliance check
