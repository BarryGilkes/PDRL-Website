About PDRL

Pro Drag Racing League — built for clean competition and clear structure.

Our Mission

PDRL was created to bring professional-level organization to grassroots drag racing. We believe that clear rules, fair competition, and transparent results make racing better for everyone — drivers, crews, and spectators alike.

Our goal is to provide a platform where racers of all skill levels can compete in a structured, safe, and exciting environment. From True Street daily drivers to full Pro builds, PDRL has a class for every level of commitment.

🎯

Fair Competition

Clear class definitions and consistent rule enforcement ensure a level playing field.

🛡️

Safety First

Comprehensive safety requirements protect our racers and spectators at every event.

📊

Transparent Results

All race times, results, and standings are published publicly for full transparency.
