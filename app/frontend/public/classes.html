Race Classes

PDRL organizes competition into four clearly defined classes across two tiers.

🏎️ True Street

Full-weight, street-legal vehicles with basic bolt-on modifications. Must be driven to the event or have current registration and insurance.

  • Full exhaust (headers/exhaust allowed)
  • Intake and tune allowed
  • Must retain full interior
  • DOT tires required
  • No slicks, no drag radials over 275

⚡ Street Mod

Modified street cars with more aggressive builds. Forced induction swaps, weight reduction, and drag radials allowed.

  • Turbo/supercharger swaps allowed
  • Weight reduction permitted
  • Drag radials allowed (any size)
  • Roll cage recommended (required if faster than 10.0)
  • Must be street-driveable

🔥 Pro Street

Purpose-built street cars with full race modifications. Slicks allowed, full cage required.

  • Slicks allowed
  • Full roll cage required
  • Any engine/drivetrain combo
  • Wheelie bars allowed
  • Must have full body panels

🏆 Pro

Top-tier competition. No restrictions on power adders, weight, or tire type. Full safety equipment mandatory.

  • No restrictions on modifications
  • Full cage, fire suit, Hans device required
  • SFI-rated equipment mandatory
  • Parachute required if faster than 7.50
  • Must pass full tech inspection

Competition Format

🎯

Heads-Up

Side-by-side racing. First to the finish line wins.

🏁

Single Elimination

Lose once and you're out. Win to advance.

📋

16-Car Bracket

Maximum 16 cars per class bracket.
